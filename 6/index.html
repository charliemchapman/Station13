6: You can't make sushi without good fish

Alex triggers a brief metaphysical digression on the nature of time and space, and Dani clarifies some of the points made last episode about internet security. Alex’s watch needs a new strap, which prompts a discussion about the pleasure to be gained by owning a mechanical watch. Alex talks about his two years working in industrial design, and how that experience has informed his approach to design in other fields, such as video games.

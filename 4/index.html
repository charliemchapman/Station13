4: You got me, guv'nor

Travel tips from Station 13! Alex shares advice he learnt during his honeymoon in Greece and Germany, and Dani talks about the best places to have a sit down and a think in Kyoto. Alex watches a video of sumo-wrestling robots, which naturally leads to a conversation about vacuum cleaners. And lastly, they discuss standing desks, and the advantages and disadvantages of open-plan offices.
